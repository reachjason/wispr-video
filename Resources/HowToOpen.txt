Wispr Video — How to open
==========================

Because this app is shared directly (not through the Mac App Store), macOS
will warn you the first time you open it. This is normal for indie apps.

To install and open:

  1. Drag "Wispr Video" onto the Applications folder (in this window).
  2. Open your Applications folder, then RIGHT-CLICK (or Control-click)
     "Wispr Video" and choose "Open".
  3. Click "Open" in the dialog. You only have to do this once.

If macOS still refuses (newer versions of macOS):

  1. Double-click the app once and dismiss the warning.
  2. Open System Settings > Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click
     "Open Anyway" next to Wispr Video. Then open the app again.

Using it:

  - The app lives in your MENU BAR (a small camera icon at the top-right
    of the screen) — there is no Dock icon.
  - Press Option-Command-V (⌥⌘V) to start/stop recording, or click the
    menu-bar icon.
  - The first time, macOS asks for camera and microphone access — click Allow.
  - After recording, pick which social formats to export. Files are saved to
    your Movies folder, under WisprVideo.

Everything runs locally on your Mac. Nothing is ever uploaded.
